Collecting and Using Your Personal Data

Types of Data Collected

XRunner VR itself does not directly request or store personal information. The only data we access comes from your Meta profile for the purpose of displaying the in-game leaderboard:

  • Your public Meta display name
  • Your public Meta profile picture/avatar
  • Your Meta account ID (hashed in-memory for ranking logic)

Use of Your Personal Data

The Meta-profile data shown above is used solely to:

  • Rank players and display names/avatars on the live leaderboard
  • Detect duplicate accounts and maintain fair competition
No other personal data is collected, and we never use this information for advertising, analytics beyond the leaderboard, or any non-gameplay purpose.

Retention of Your Personal Data

XRunner VR does not persist Meta-profile data to disk or cloud storage. All data is kept in volatile memory for the duration of your play session and is permanently discarded when you exit the game.

Disclosure of Your Personal Data

We do not share, sell, or otherwise transfer any leaderboard or profile information to third parties. Rankings are shown only to players inside the game session.

Security of Your Personal Data

Even though we do not store personal data long-term, we implement industry-standard security measures (encrypted transport, secure sandboxing, least-privilege access) to protect the transient data handled during gameplay.

Children’s Privacy

Our Service is not directed to anyone under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you become aware that a child has provided us personal data, please contact us so we can delete it immediately.
